The Fix and The Warning

What happens when two men—one a revolutionary atheist, the other a reforming Anabaptist—confront the same broken world with radically different remedies? The Fix and The Warning places Karl Marx and Samuel Froehlich in rare and revealing conversation, not to draw simplistic contrasts, but to illuminate two divergent paths out of cultural crisis: one through political revolution, the other through spiritual renewal.

Drawing from history, theology, and personal conviction, this work challenges modern readers to consider not just what must be fixed in our world—but how we are called to live in response. In a time obsessed with systemic change, Froehlich’s quiet resistance through faith, community, and separation from power feels more urgent—and more radical—than ever.
